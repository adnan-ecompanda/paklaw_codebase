THE PRIVY PURSES (CHARGED EXPENDITURE) ACT, 1968 CONTENTS 1 Short title 2 Expenditure on privy purse to be charged expenditure THE PRIVY PURSES (CHARGED EXPENDITURE) ACT, 1968 Act No. IX of 1968 [7th June, 1968] An Act to declare the expenditure relating to privy purses of Rulers of certain former States to be a charge on the Central Consolidated Fund WHEREAS it is expedient to declare the expenditure relating to privy purses of Rulers of certain former States to be a charge on the Central Consolidated Fund; It is hereby enacted as follows:- 1. Short title. This Act may be called the Privy Purses (Charged Expenditure) Act, 1968. 2. Expenditure on privy purse to be charged expenditure.-Where, under any agreement for the time being in force between the 1[Federal Government] and the Ruler of a former State which at any time was in accession with Pakistan or his successor, any sum is payable as privy purse by the 1[Federal Government], such sum shall be charged on the 1[Federal Consolidated Fund] and be paid out of that Fund and shall be deemed always to have been so charged and paid.  1Subs. by the F.A.O., 1975 (P.O.4)., Art. 2 and Table.
