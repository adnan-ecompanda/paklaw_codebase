THE REVOCATION OF PRIVILEGES ACT, 1992 CONTENTS 1. Short title and commencement 2. Annulment of certain Bills THE REVOCATION OF PRIVILEGES ACT, 1992 ACT No. VIII of 1992 An Act to revoke and annual certain Bills passed by the National Assembly WHEREAS it is expedient to revoke and annual the Bills passed by the National Assembly on the 3rd day of May, 1992; AND WHEREAS the National Assembly unanimously by a Resolution on May, 7, 1992, resolved to withdraw the said Bills; It is hereby enacted as follows :⸻ 1. Short title and commencement.-(1) This Act may be called the Revocation of Privileges Act, 1992. (2) It shall come into force at once and shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 3rd day of May, 1992. 2. Annulment of certain Bills.⸻ Notwithstanding anything contained in any other law or the rules for the time being in force, the President’s Salary, Allowances and Privileges (Amendment) Bill, 1992, the Prime Minister’s Salary, Allowances and Privileges (Amendment) Bill, 1992, the Federal Ministers and Ministers of State Salaries, Allowances and Privileges (Amendment) Bill, 1992, the Members of Parliament (Salaries and Allowances) (Amendment) Bill, 1992, the Chairman and Speaker (Salaries, Allowances and Privileges) (Amendment) Bill, 1992, and the Deputy Chairman and Deputy Speaker (Salaries, Allowances and Privileges) (Amendment) Bill, 1992, passed by the National Assembly of Pakistan on the 3rd day of May, 1992, resolved to be withdrawn by a unanimous Resolution of the National Assembly, whether transmitted to the President for his assent or not, or whether deemed to have become Acts of Parliament by afflux of time or not, are withdrawn, revoked and annulled and shall be deemed always to have been withdrawn, revoked and annulled and of no legal effect.
